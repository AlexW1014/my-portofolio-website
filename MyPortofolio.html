Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is an advanced, strategic twist on the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game. In this version, the board is composed of nine smaller 3x3 grids arranged in a larger 3x3 grid. The objective remains the same—get three in a row—but with an added layer of complexity. Lexia Learn

"Lexia Learn" is an e-learning platform designed for children with Surface Dyslexia. It uses phonics-based solo and multiplayer games to assist with reading, writing, and spelling difficulties through engaging activities like word play, letter blocks, and animations. This project was a group effort with two other teammates and was developed for the Gemastik Software Development Competition. My role in the project was primarily as a designer.
